Writing

"In the world according to her father, Jenny Garp knew, we must have energy."

This is a quote from my favourite book, The World According to Garp by John Irving. This was the book that made me want to write fiction, among other pieces.

I have self-published a book (and like many self-published books, it sold next to nothing), been longlisted for a short story prize, and am in the midst of working on hopefully my first professionally published novel.

Recently I received a highly commended short story entry in the 2021 Sutherland Shire Literary Competition.

Stayed tuned for that dream to come true, but for now, check out my blog. I write short stories, vignettes, and flash fiction, as well as my fair share of satirical pieces, that are often mistaken for the real thing.

I also write a tiny a story a day and post on Facebook, Instagram, Reddit and Twitter as nanonarratives, @nanonarratives, /r/NanoNarratives/ and @narrativesnano respectively. Give 'em a follow.
